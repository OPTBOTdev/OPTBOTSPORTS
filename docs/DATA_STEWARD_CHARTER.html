Role Charter — Data Assurance & Product Surfaces

Mission of the role: while Griffin builds and trains the models, YOU own two things: (1) the truth of every number that enters or leaves the system — features, data, confounders — and (2) the surfaces that show that truth to the world. The model can only be as honest as the data steward. That's you.

Context in one line: the system beats the industry projection by 7.6% on 860 frozen-clock historical moves with 80.0% measured band coverage — every claim regenerable from numbered scripts in this repo. Read README.md (the whitepaper) and docs/ARCHITECTURE_PLAN.md first. Onboarding exercises: docs/HANDOFF.html Part 1 — do those before anything below.

DUTY 2 — Custodian of the Confounder Register (T1–T23)

The threat register in README.md §2 + ARCHITECTURE_PLAN.md §6 is the company's causal immune system: 23 named threats, each SOLVED / MEASURED / PRICED / GATED / OPEN. You keep it TRUE:

  • Re-verify: independently reproduce the evidence behind 5 threats per week until you've touched all 23 (each has scripts/artifacts cited). Fresh-eyes disagreement = escalate.
  • Keep current: every pipeline change, new feature, or new season gets a "which threats does this touch?" note in the PR. You're the reviewer who asks it.
  • Standing sensitivity suite (own the re-runs): survivorship filter at ≥10 GP vs ≥20 (T4) · era slices of the headline · per-cell conformal coverage (n_eff × role-delta — the K-2 finding) · MP-vs-inhouse xG drift (the witness aux) · rink-bias trends (T19).
  • Red-team quarterly: half a day, adversarial: "how would I fake this result?" — written findings, even (especially) when the answer is 'I couldn't'.

Deliverable: monthly one-page "Register Health" note — statuses, re-runs done, anything that moved.

DUTY 3 — Continuous Data QA (the 2025-26 lesson, institutionalized)

This season we found: empty shiftchart files from API outages, 505 silently-hollow game files, a whole season with zeroed outcomes. All caught by gates — gates you now operate:

  • Every rebuild runs the regression suite: vendor/window_engine/README.md gate list + scripts/09 (official records) + scripts/10 (crown jewel) + battery in scripts/16. Zero-issue baselines are documented; deviations block.
  • New-season ingestion (2026-27, from October): you own collection health — file sizes, stub detection, marker discipline, shiftchart emptiness (the HTML-report fallback in html_shift_fallback.py is certified for API gaps).
  • Nightly during the season: the actuals-scoring job for the public scoreboard (Duty 4) doubles as a data canary — anomalies there are your first alert.
