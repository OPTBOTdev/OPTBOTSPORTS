Feature & Data Audit — Role Brief

Your job in one sentence: verify every number our models eat, and evaluate the numbers we DON'T have yet — how easy to get, and whether they matter for the MVP.

Context: the system already beats the industry projection by 7.6% on 860 historical moves, with error bars measured at 80.0% accuracy. The MVP bar: enough proof to land a partnership with an NHL team or seed funding — that's it. Not perfection; provable credibility. Your work is a direct part of that proof ("every feature independently audited by a second engineer" is a sentence that closes meetings).

Your two manuals (keep these files in the same folder as this one, or open them from the repo docs/):
📖 AUDITOR_GUIDE.html — datasets & paths (pbp = source of truth), the Window Key, the script index, the Feature Key, and each check below in plain English with a copy-paste command.
📖 PHASES_EXPLAINED.html — what Phases A/B/C do in plain language, what each feature's job is, and the MVP triage rule.
